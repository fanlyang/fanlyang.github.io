Research Experience

MPI-based Parallelization and Optimization of a Classical Molecular Dynamics Solver

  • Hand-coded an MD solver from scratch in C++ (including the Velocity-Verlet integrator). Designed and implemented a robust Domain Decomposition strategy using MPI for efficient load balancing.
  • Optimized short-range force calculations by leveraging the Cell List method and a 26Neighbor Cell Communication Scheme, significantly minimizing MPI overhead and demonstrating high parallel scalability on HPC clusters.

Quantum-Classical Hybrid Computing for Linear Systems

  • Conducted a comparative study of hybrid quantum-classical approaches to solving the linear system Ax=b by implementing both the HHL and VQLS algorithms.
  • Utilized the XACC Framework to orchestrate hybrid workflows, integrating multiple quantum backends.
  • Performed detailed performance benchmarking, focusing on low-level compilation, IR transformations, and optimization beyond high-level Python SDKs.

Object-Oriented Development of an Advanced Nonlinear FEM Solver in Java

  • Engineered an object-oriented FEM solver from scratch in Java, establishing a modular architecture for linear 1D structural elements with post-processing visualization.
  • Architecture emphasizes abstraction and polymorphism for flexible integration of new elements, material models, and boundary conditions.
  • Implemented advanced nonlinear solvers, including Newton–Raphson iteration and generalized arc-length method, achieving robust convergence in complex post-buckling phenomena.

AI for Scientific Computation (Coming Soon)
