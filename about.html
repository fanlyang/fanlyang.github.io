About Me

I am Fan Yang, currently pursuing a Master in Computational Engineering. My research focuses on accelerating scientific computations using HPC, Quantum Computing, and AI-based surrogate models. Previously, I implemented FEM solvers for engineering problems, parallelized molecular dynamics simulations using MPI, and explored hybrid quantum-classical algorithms for linear systems. My goal is to integrate these approaches to solve complex multi-scale problems efficiently.
